## Briefing: Through the Back Door uwu

**Context:**

A wave of credit card fraud is affecting thousands of citizens across the country. Reports indicate a coordinated pattern: victims receive text messages (smishing) that impersonate banks, airlines, and department stores, requesting confirmation of personal data. Shortly after, their cards are cloned and used for fraudulent purchases at businesses in Playa del Crimen.

**Mission:**

As an intelligence team, your mission is to:

1. **Identify the infrastructure** used by the criminal group.
2. **Map the network of actors** involved in the operation.
3. **Analyze the group's modus operandi**, from smishing to money laundering.
4. **Assess the economic and social impact** of the fraud.
5. **Propose countermeasures** to dismantle the operation and protect citizens.

**Learning Objectives:**

- **C2 infrastructure analysis:** How to identify domains, IPs, and servers used in smishing campaigns.
- **Cryptocurrency tracking techniques:** How to follow the flow of money on the blockchain.
- **Human intelligence (HUMINT):** How to analyze clandestine forums and dark web markets.
- **Reputational risk analysis:** How to assess the impact of fraud on financial and commercial institutions.
