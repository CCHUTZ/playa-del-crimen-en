# Team Structure: The Ghost Train

This team reflects real structures of strategic information operations.

- **Information Strategy Director:** Defines narrative objectives, priority audiences, desired political outcomes.
- **Social Sentiment Intelligence Analyst:** Analyzes social media conversations, public opinion trends, prevailing emotions. Identifies sensitive topics, most influenceable groups.
- **Narrative Mapping Specialist:** Builds maps of dominant narratives, recurring arguments, influential actors in the discourse.
- **Behavioral Influence Analyst:** Studies which messages generate the most reaction, which emotions mobilize the population, which narrative frameworks change perceptions.
- **Network Amplification Analyst:** Identifies key dissemination nodes, social influencers, active digital communities.
