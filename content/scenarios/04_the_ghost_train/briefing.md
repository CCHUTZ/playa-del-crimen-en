## Briefing: The Ghost Train

**Context:**

A controversial mega-project, a high-speed train, is facing widespread public opposition. The government has launched a massive public relations campaign to win over public opinion, but there are suspicions that the campaign is using disinformation and manipulation tactics.

**Mission:**

As an intelligence team, your mission is to:

1. **Analyze the government's PR campaign** and identify any disinformation or manipulation tactics.
2. **Assess the impact of the campaign** on public opinion.
3. **Identify the key actors** behind the campaign.
4. **Propose a strategy** to counter the disinformation and promote an informed public debate.

**Learning Objectives:**

- **Informational influence analysis:** How to analyze and counter disinformation campaigns.
- **Public opinion analysis:** How to assess and understand public opinion.
- **Narrative analysis:** How to identify and analyze the narratives used in influence campaigns.
- **Strategic communication:** How to develop and implement effective communication strategies.
