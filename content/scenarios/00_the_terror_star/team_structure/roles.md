# Team Structure: The Terror Star

This team combines cybersecurity, financial analysis, and OSINT skills.

- **Cyber Threat Analyst:** Investigates the group's technical infrastructure (domains, malware, etc.).
- **Forensic Financial Analyst:** Traces the flow of money, from fraudulent purchases to asset laundering.
- **OSINT Analyst:** Collects and analyzes open-source information on suspects and their activities.
- **Social Engineering Specialist:** Analyzes the smishing and manipulation tactics used by the group.
- **Dark Web Analyst:** Monitors clandestine forums and markets for relevant information.
