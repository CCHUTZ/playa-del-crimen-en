# Team Structure: Sand Castles

This team combines digital forensics, behavioral analysis, and fugitive tracking expertise.

- **Digital Forensics Analyst:** Traces the developer's digital footprint and recovers evidence.
- **Behavioral Analyst:** Analyzes the developer's behavioral patterns and predicts their next move.
- **Network Analyst:** Maps and analyzes the developer's network of associates and shell companies.
- **OSINT Analyst:** Collects and analyzes open-source information to locate the developer.
- **Fugitive Recovery Specialist:** Coordinates the effort to apprehend the developer.
