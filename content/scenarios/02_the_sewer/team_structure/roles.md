# Team Structure: The Sewer

This team combines political analysis, financial investigation, and public records expertise.

- **Political Analyst:** Investigates the political context of the corruption and the actors involved.
- **Financial Investigator:** Traces the flow of illicit funds and identifies shell companies.
- **Public Records Specialist:** Analyzes public records to uncover evidence of corruption.
- **OSINT Analyst:** Collects and analyzes open-source information on all parties involved.
- **Investigative Journalist:** Synthesizes the findings and prepares them for public disclosure.
