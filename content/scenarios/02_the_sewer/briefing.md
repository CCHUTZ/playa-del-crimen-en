## Briefing: The Sewer

**Context:**

A series of investigative reports reveals a network of corruption in the municipality of Playa del Crimen. Public funds intended for infrastructure projects have been diverted to shell companies controlled by local politicians and their associates.

**Mission:**

As an intelligence team, your mission is to:

1. **Trace the flow of public funds** from the municipality to the shell companies.
2. **Identify the politicians and associates** involved in the corruption network.
3. **Analyze the modus operandi** of the network, from bid-rigging to money laundering.
4. **Assess the political and social impact** of the corruption.
5. **Propose a strategy** to expose the network and bring the perpetrators to justice.

**Learning Objectives:**

- **Political intelligence:** How to investigate and analyze political corruption.
- **Financial intelligence:** How to trace the flow of illicit funds.
- **Network analysis:** How to map and analyze complex corruption networks.
- **Public records analysis:** How to use public records to uncover evidence of corruption.
