# Playa del Crimen

**OSINT Training Scenarios**

Playa del Crimen is an educational project designed to teach OSINT (Open Source Intelligence), threat intelligence, and sociotechnical security through immersive, narrative-driven training scenarios.

## 🎯 Philosophy

The best way to learn defense is to understand offense. Each scenario is a deep dive into the tactics, techniques, and procedures (TTPs) used by real-world threat actors, presented in a safe and ethical educational environment.

## 📚 Scenarios

- **Scenario 00: The Terror Star** - Learn how a sophisticated threat actor could dismantle a powerful, informal organization by exploiting its human vulnerabilities.
- **Scenario 01: Operation Krakatoa** - Explore how to investigate complex corporate ecosystems and detect reputational vulnerabilities in environmental conflicts.
- **Scenario 02: The Sewer** - Discover how to trace the flow of public resources and expose municipal corruption through political intelligence.
- **Scenario 03: Sand Castles** - Learn digital identity tracking and behavioral profiling techniques in real estate fraud cases.
- **Scenario 04: The Ghost Train** - Understand how informational influence campaigns operate and analyze public opinion at scale.
- **Scenario 05: Through the Back Door uwu** - Immerse yourself in the world of carding, smishing, and financial fraud operations.

This project is designed for cybersecurity professionals, intelligence analysts, red teamers, security educators, and business leaders who want to understand the real-world risks that organizations face in the digital age.

Playa del Crimen is a fictional place. Any resemblance to real people, places, or events is purely coincidental. All scenarios are for educational purposes only.
