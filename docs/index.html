About the Project

Who is this for?

Playa del Crimen is designed for cybersecurity professionals, intelligence analysts, red teams, security educators, and business leaders who need to understand the real risks organizations face in the digital age.

Our philosophy is direct: the best defense is built by understanding the attack. Each scenario is an immersion into the tactics, techniques, and procedures (TTPs) used by real-world threat actors, presented in a controlled, ethically responsible educational environment.

No shortcuts. No easy answers. Just applied intelligence.
