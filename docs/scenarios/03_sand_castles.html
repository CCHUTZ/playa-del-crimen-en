Scenario 03: Sand Castles

Adversary intelligence in real estate fraud and fugitive tracking

This scenario immerses you in digital identity tracking and behavioral profiling techniques in real estate fraud cases.

Briefing: Sand Castles

Context:

A real estate developer has defrauded hundreds of investors in a luxury housing project in Playa del Crimen. The developer has disappeared with the investors' money, leaving behind a trail of shell companies and false identities.

Mission:

As an intelligence team, your mission is to:

  1. Trace the developer's digital footprint and identify their true identity.
  2. Reconstruct the developer's network of associates and shell companies.
  3. Analyze the developer's behavioral patterns to predict their next move.
  4. Locate the developer and bring them to justice.

Learning Objectives:

  • Digital identity tracking: How to trace and analyze digital footprints.
  • Behavioral profiling: How to analyze behavioral patterns to predict future actions.
  • Network analysis: How to map and analyze complex criminal networks.
  • Fugitive tracking: How to use OSINT to locate fugitives.

Team Structure: Sand Castles

This team combines digital forensics, behavioral analysis, and fugitive tracking expertise.

  • Digital Forensics Analyst: Traces the developer's digital footprint and recovers evidence.
  • Behavioral Analyst: Analyzes the developer's behavioral patterns and predicts their next move.
  • Network Analyst: Maps and analyzes the developer's network of associates and shell companies.
  • OSINT Analyst: Collects and analyzes open-source information to locate the developer.
  • Fugitive Recovery Specialist: Coordinates the effort to apprehend the developer.
