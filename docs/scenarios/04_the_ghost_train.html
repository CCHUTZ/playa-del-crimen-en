Scenario 04: The Ghost Train

Adversary intelligence in public opinion and large-scale political projects

This scenario immerses you in the world of informational influence campaigns and large-scale public opinion analysis.

Briefing: The Ghost Train

Context:

A controversial mega-project, a high-speed train, is facing widespread public opposition. The government has launched a massive public relations campaign to win over public opinion, but there are suspicions that the campaign is using disinformation and manipulation tactics.

Mission:

As an intelligence team, your mission is to:

  1. Analyze the government's PR campaign and identify any disinformation or manipulation tactics.
  2. Assess the impact of the campaign on public opinion.
  3. Identify the key actors behind the campaign.
  4. Propose a strategy to counter the disinformation and promote an informed public debate.

Learning Objectives:

  • Informational influence analysis: How to analyze and counter disinformation campaigns.
  • Public opinion analysis: How to assess and understand public opinion.
  • Narrative analysis: How to identify and analyze the narratives used in influence campaigns.
  • Strategic communication: How to develop and implement effective communication strategies.

Team Structure: The Ghost Train

This team reflects real structures of strategic information operations.

  • Information Strategy Director: Defines narrative objectives, priority audiences, desired political outcomes.
  • Social Sentiment Intelligence Analyst: Analyzes social media conversations, public opinion trends, prevailing emotions. Identifies sensitive topics, most influenceable groups.
  • Narrative Mapping Specialist: Builds maps of dominant narratives, recurring arguments, influential actors in the discourse.
  • Behavioral Influence Analyst: Studies which messages generate the most reaction, which emotions mobilize the population, which narrative frameworks change perceptions.
  • Network Amplification Analyst: Identifies key dissemination nodes, social influencers, active digital communities.
